Conference Fee

The conference fee is 25 Euros and includes:

  • Accommodation from June 28th to July 2nd
  • Breakfast at the accommodation
  • Lunch in the Mensa on campus on Thursday and Friday, catering on Saturday

All meals include vegan and vegetarian options. Participation in the Sunday Brunch is not included.
